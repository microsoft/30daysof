Welcome!‚Äã

Hello and welcome to the launch of the #30DaysOf learning hub. üëãüèΩ

This is a collaborative effort within Developer Relations to collect and share various resources that support self-study and structured learning for beginners and experienced devs alike. Technology changes fast! The only way to adapt, and thrive, is to learn continuously - and apply learnings to practical problems.

So, what does that learning journey look like? Think of it as four stages:

  • Welcome - the onboarding phase where you get setup for the journey.
  • Guide - the resource exploration phase, where you pick the path to travel.
  • Connect - the community phase where you interact with others on the same journey.
  • Share - the pay-it-forward phase where you share your learnings to help the next traveller.

About The Site‚Äã

Let's take a quick tour of this site and the various features it offers:

  • Landing Page - explore the learning options available.
  • Blog - subscribe to the feed for announcements & updates
  • Calendar - discover upcoming events, revisit archived ones
  • Roadmaps - discover and complete #30Day journeys
  • Curricula - explore open-source multi-week courses with lesson plans
  • Challenges - join challenges and compete with your peers
  • Discussions - ask questions, interact with peers, show-and-tell!

Your First Actions‚Äã

Where should you start?

  1. Take a minute to introduce yourself - What are you looking to learn? What are you an expert in? Which region are you joining us from?
  2. Check the calendar - look for upcoming or in-progress learning journeys (e.g., #30Days) and join in! Work at your own pace, or look for events and discussions where you can interact with other learners.
  3. Cultivate learning habits - Use techniques that help you learn to learn better. Take breaks, switch between focused and relaxed modes, practice what you learn, and understand that each person learns differently.

Most importantly, set a goal and keep going towards it one learning step at a time - connect with others to get help or feel inspired. Then pay it forward by sharing blog posts and showcasing your apps, so others can learn from you.

We can't wait to see what you learn - next!
